Tied up in probate?

KEEP your inherited California home

The Problem:

You've inherited a California home or rental property and want to keep it, at least for now. But you've also inherited your share of the debts, expenses and problems that make it hard to imagine how you'd ever pay the bills before the house is finally in your name.

The Solution:

We make mortgage loans secured by your California property during probate or trust administration, so that the person in charge (administrator or executor) can keep the home in the family . . . for now or forever, even when others are pushing to sell.

If you are inheriting all or a large portion of a California property, you may prefer to just sell your share early, too.
